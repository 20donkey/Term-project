Term Project Proposal - HungryAsians

What is this website about?

This website will introduce must-eat asian restaurants in Boston. There will be some basic information about each restaurants such as their most popular meals, average price, whether you need a researvation. There will also be link to their offical websites for more information or researcation.

Why this website?

I have spent four years trying and visting almost every asian restaurants in Boston. If new asian students have no idea of where to eat, this website will give them some ideas.

Who should use this website?

New students studying in boston areas especially asians or people who love asian food.
